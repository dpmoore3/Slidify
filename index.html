Temperature Conversion

Dave Moore

Purpose

Have you ever traveled to a place that expresses temperature in different units than you are accustomed to?

I am from the United States where temperature is typically expressed in degrees Fahrenheit. In most other countries that I have traveled to, temperature is expressed in degrees Celsius. When traveling, I often do the temperature conversion in my head.

This cutting-edge app will do the conversion for you! It converts both from Celsius to Fahrenheit and from Fahrenheit to Celsius.

How to Use the App

  1. Enter the temperature you want converted.
  2. Enter the type of units that temperature is expressed in (c for Celsius and f for Fahrenheit).
  3. Press submit.
  4. The app will return the converted temperature (if you entered c for Celsius, it will return the Fahrenheit conversion and vice-versa)

It's that simple!

Converting Celsius to Fahrenheit

The secret behind this miracle app is in the formula below:

To convert from Celsius to Fahrenheit, perform the following calculation:

Fahrenheit = Celsius*9/5 + 32

Converting Fahrenheit to Celsius

An equally profound secret is the following formula to convert Fahrenheit to Celsius:

Celsius = (Fahrenheit - 32)*5/9
